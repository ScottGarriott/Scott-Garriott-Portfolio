Welcome to my webpage!

I have a keen eye for detail and I love learning new things. I am a great team player, one has to be if they want to DM. In my spare time I play table top role playing games. It is a great way to collaborate with your friends and make an interesting and compelling story at the same time. It gives me a performative outlet, which I was missing after I stopped acting after high school.

Here are a few ways I think being a dungeon master can help me with my web design career:

  • It has prepared me to think on my feet. Being a DM is all about being adaptable, players always do what you are not expecting.
  • It has helped me learn to manage a group of people so everyone gets thier ideas heard.
  • Having started our game during the pandemic I have gotten alot better with general file organization. You gotta have all the characters and locations easily accessable!
